Not long after I arrived, the ██████████ came. They took a really really long time: examining me, measuring me, naming me. They had names for parts of me I didn't even know existed. They measured those meticulously and drew... conclusions. But there was one part of me that troubled them deeply. They didn't examine it, they didn't measure it, they didn't name it.

They said it wasn't there.
